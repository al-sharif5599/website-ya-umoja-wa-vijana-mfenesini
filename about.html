About Us

We are a youth cooperative in Mfenesini, Zanzibar. Our focus is building skills, creating opportunities, and strengthening community bonds.

MFENESINI YOUTH DEVELOPMENT COOPERATIVE

We mobilize young people to improve livelihoods and wellbeing through practical community service. From agriculture to education support, we keep our work visible, measurable, and community-driven.

Mission

To empower youth through organized community service and capacity building.

Vision

A resilient community where youth lead sustainable development initiatives.

Objectives

Deliver community activities, support education, encourage sports and unity, and promote entrepreneurship.

Community Impact

Our activities strengthen social cohesion, improve local environments, and create pathways for young leaders.
